Burt Malkiel
Chief Investment Officer, PhD

About Burt
Burt Malkiel, Professor of Economics, Emeritus, and Senior Economist at Princeton University, is Wealthfront’s Chief Investment Officer. Burt is the author of A Random Walk Down Wall Street, which launched the passive investing revolution four decades ago. He has served as a past appointee to the President's Council of Economic Advisor and a past board member of the Vanguard Group. In his free time, Burt enjoys watching WWE and trying new restaurants.
