Burt Malkiel
Chief Investment Officer, PhD

About Burt
Burt Malkiel, Professor of Economics, Emeritus, and Senior Economist at Princeton University, is Wealthfront’s Chief Investment Officer. Burt is the author of A Random Walk Down Wall Street, which launched the passive investing revolution four decades ago. He has served as a past appointee to the President's Council of Economic Advisor and a past board member of the Vanguard Group. In his free time, Burt enjoys watching WWE and trying new restaurants.

Celine Sun
Director, PhD, CFA

About Celine
Celine Sun is the Director of Research at Wealthfront and focuses on developing our investment strategy. Celine earned her PhD in Financial Economics from the University of Washington and BS in Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China. She is a CFA charterholder and member of the CFA Society of San Francisco. In her downtime, Celine enjoys hiking in the Bay Area and cooking with her family.
